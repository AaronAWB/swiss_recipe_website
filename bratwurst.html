Bratwurse mit Zweibelsauce (Bratwurst with Onion Sauce)

Description

Delicious Swiss bratwurst in rich onion sauce with a side of spatzle. A classic Swiss dish!

Ingredients

  • knob of butter
  • 600 g onions, chopped in rings
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 200 ml beer
  • 200 ml broth
  • salt and pepper
  • 4 bratwurst

Directions

  1. In a large frying pan, heat the butter until spluttering.

  2. Add the onions and cook for about 5 minutes, or until the are soft and translucent. Sprinkle over the flour, stir until the onions are coated, fry a minute more, then deglaze the pan with the beer and broth. Season with salt and pepper.

  3. Turn the heat down low and simmer the sauce while you fry or grill your Bratwurst.

  4. Once the sausages are ready, place them on your plates and smother with onion sauce.
